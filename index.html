About the School

About Us: Narsingdi Government Girls High School, nestled in the heart of Shatirpara, Narsingdi, stands as a beacon of educational excellence since its founding in 1936. With a rich legacy of fostering young girls, our institution is dedicated to not just academic success but holistic personal growth. Here, education transcends textbooks; it inspires confidence, creativity, and a spirit of service, making our school a cherished haven for learning.

Vision & Mission: Our vision is to empower every girl through transformative education, nurturing them into self-assured and responsible citizens of the future. We strive to create a vibrant learning environment that promotes critical thinking, ethical values, and a commitment to community service, preparing our students to face the world with integrity and resilience.

Achievements: Our school has consistently received recognition for outstanding academic performance and vibrant participation in cultural and sports events. These accolades reflect our unwavering commitment to excellence in education and the all-round development of our students.

Principal: Under the leadership of Md. Abdul Ahed, our principal, the school continues to uphold its values and mission, ensuring that every student receives the support and guidance needed for success.

Admission Process

Admission Requirements: To join our esteemed institution, prospective students must meet the age criteria and provide essential documents, including birth certificates and previous educational records.

Application Process: Interested applicants are invited to complete the application form, attach the required documents, and submit them to our administration office. We ensure a smooth and supportive admission process for all new entrants.

Important Dates: Stay informed about admission deadlines and open days, typically announced at the beginning of each academic session. We encourage parents and students to keep an eye on our official notices for updates.
